Fun Facts about the Blue Jay
  • Being founded in 1977 The Toronto Blue Jays played their first game on April 7, 1977
  • The blue jays were back to back World Series champions in the 1992 and 1993 seasions.
  • The Blue Jays are the first and only team outside of the USA to appear in and win a World Series.
